about us

Our Story

Ascent Décor is amongst the leading home furnishing fabric manufacturers in India. Our products range across jacquards, plains & textures, upholsteries, velvets, digital prints, screen prints, block prints, simple as well as decorative sheers and widest range of embroideries.

Our Brands

The company houses multiple brands and aims to provide fabrics to all ranges from high end niche products, mid segment products, as well as value line / low cost economical range for both domestic and international market.
